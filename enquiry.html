Get Involved With Us

At the Trevor Noah Foundation, we believe that change starts with people. Whether you’d like to volunteer your time, sponsor a program, or simply learn more, your contribution helps us reach more young people across South Africa.

Why Get Involved?

  • Volunteers – Mentor and inspire students, support events, and be part of grassroots change.
  • Sponsors – Help us expand our reach by providing resources and funding for schools and leadership programs.
  • Supporters – Spread awareness of our mission and contribute in ways that matter to you.
