About Us

Our History

The Trevor Noah Foundation was established in South Africa by comedian and philanthropist Trevor Noah. Drawing from his own experiences of growing up in a challenging environment, Trevor created the foundation to bridge the gaps in education and empower young people to succeed.

Our Mission

To provide equitable access to education, skills, and leadership opportunities for youth across South Africa.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every young person has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Team

Our passionate team includes educators, community leaders, and youth advocates working together to create lasting impact.
