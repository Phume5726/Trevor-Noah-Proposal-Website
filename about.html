About Us

Our History

The Trevor Noah Foundation was established to address challenges in South Africa’s education system. Inspired by Trevor Noah’s personal journey, the foundation focuses on creating opportunities for young people.

Our Mission

To improve education and leadership opportunities for underserved youth in South Africa.

Our Vision

A future where every young person can access quality education and achieve their full potential.

Our Impact

Over 100 schools supported and thousands of students reached across South Africa through programs and partnerships.

Our Team

We are supported by passionate educators, community leaders, and volunteers who work together to make change possible.
